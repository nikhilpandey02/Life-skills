# Focus Management

### Q1: What is Deep Work?  
- Deep Work = working with full focus.  
- No distractions.  
- Helps do better work.  

### Q2: How to do Deep Work properly?  
- Stop distractions (phone, social media).  
- Fix a time to do deep work.  
- Work, then take a break.
